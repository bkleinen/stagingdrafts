Preparation

Monday

10 a.m.

  • Explain the Sprint (10min?)

10:15-ish

  • Set a long-term goal
  • List Sprint Questions (30min?)

11:30-ish

  • Make a map: use The Design Sprint Note-n-Map - (45min?)

  • Lunch Break
